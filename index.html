This site is meant to give a multidisciplinary study of The Lion King. It gives an introduction to different types of government, sociology, psychology, and law using The Lion King, a commonly known childhood movie, as its core. This efficiently bridges the gap between fun, nostalgic films and applicable subjects. For each discipline, we have a four to six minute video, lesson plan directed at middle-high school level students, worksheet, and links to external resources for more information.
