Government

By Alexander High

This video essay explores the different types of government as seen in The Lion King. It first explores the type of government used by Simba and Mufasa, which is either a monarchy or an aparthiedistic state. It then explores the type of government used by Scar, which is either a communist of a fascist one. It gives a few examples supporting each case, but ultimately leaves the final decision up to the viewer.
